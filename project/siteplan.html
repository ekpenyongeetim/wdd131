Site Plan: My Digital Portfolio

Site Name

Site Name: My Digital Portfolio

Reason for Selection: The name is simple, descriptive, and aligns with the purpose of showcasing my web development skills and projects professionally.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to showcase my skills, projects, and achievements in web development. It will provide potential clients and employers with a clear understanding of my expertise, career journey, and the ability to contact me for collaborations or job opportunities.

Scenarios

  • Who is the person behind this portfolio, and what is their professional expertise?
  • How can I explore their web development projects and view live demos or code repositories?
  • What is the best way to contact them for a project or job opportunity?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #007acc (Blue) - Used for headings, buttons, and navigation links.
  • Secondary Color: #f4f4f4 (Light Gray) - Used as the background color to enhance readability.
  • Accent Color: #333 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text and details.

Typography

  • Headings: Roboto, sans-serif
  • Body Text: Arial, sans-serif
